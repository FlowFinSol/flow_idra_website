OUR SERVICES

Market Research

Insights into why things happen, Foresight to see what lies ahead and Innovative solutions to tackle development problems.

Our Research Unit offers full-scale market research services. Our solutions include quantitative and qualitative research products.

The methods used include Face-to-Face, CATI, CAWI, Focus Groups, In-depth Interviews, Product Testing, Mystery Shopping, etc.

Media Intelligence

Professional media monitoring and advertising intelligence solutions in Vietnam.

We cover TV, Print, Radio, Online and OOH media and help clients interpret trends and information so they can improve their media strategies.

Through our web-based monitoring platforms clients can follow any media channels on real-time, view latest ads or news content, check the ad activity of competition, verify airing of own spots, and download up-to-the-minute reports.

Software Developement
