QUANTITATIVE RESEARCH

IDRA Research & Consulting offers quantitative research services in the form of surveys both ad-hoc and continuous (omnibus, tracking, etc)

Each project is carefully considered to respond to client needs. IDRA offers a full package of quantitative research services:

  • Research and survey design
  • Sample design
  • Questionnaire design
  • Fieldwork (PAPI, CAPI, CATI,CAWI etc)
  • Data entry and validation
  • Data analysis
  • Reporting ( in-depth analysis, graphics, summary, recommendations)
  • Presentations (media, conference, workshop)

Our work is conducted in accordance with ESOMAR Code of research practice. Our interviewers are well trained and experienced and abide by standards of IDRA IQCS (Interviewer Quality Control Scheme).
